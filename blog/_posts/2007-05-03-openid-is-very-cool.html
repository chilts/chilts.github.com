I've just signed up for my first OpenID. It's nice.

The best thing about it is that I'm using this site http://kapiti.geek.nz/ (deprecated) as a delegate to my OpenID provider. That way, if I ever want to move to another provider, I just have to change some of information in the <head> section of my index page and we're done.

Since then, I've used my new OpenID to:

  • add a comment to a LiveJournal blog - http://jenny-1260.livejournal.com/
  • sign in to http://ma.gnolia.com/
  • soon, I will be claiming blogs at http://technorati.com/
  • (probably lots of other stuff too)

It's very cool.

If you want to use your own site for your OpenID go to this help page - Using your own URL.

Finally, I might try and add OpenID comments to http://zaapt.org.nz/ (deprecated) or even make it (and hence KiwiWriters) become an OpenID Consumer! Now that would be something special.
