...or should that be 'Unleashed'

About a year ago, I wrote a progam called PollPod which I uploaded to SourceForge. It did what I wanted and more, but with not really liking the SF interface and the fact that I didn't have an MP3 player, I let it rot. A little, okay, maybe a lot.

Since I bought my Sandisk Sansa e270, I decided to give it a bit of spit and polish and resurrect it for use on my new toy. Now it is simpler, has a proper manual and even better than all that, has Debian packaging :-)

So I had registered the PollPod name at Google Code (and accepted the transfer from SF (which worked fine). After a few days of cleaning up and clearing out, I have now uploaded v0.1 and created a branch and tag for it - oh how Subversion is so nice compared to CVS.

Anyways, head over to the previously mentioned Google Code page or take a look at the PollPod Discuss or PollPod Commit lists.

Right then. After a good day of getting myself together and uploading it all, I'm off to listen to Chris Evans!
